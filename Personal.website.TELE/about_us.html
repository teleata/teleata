    About Us

    ABOUT J&J COSMETIC

    J&J Cosmetic was established in Kingston, Jamaica in 2000 by Teleata Mitchell who always had a passion for beauty and elegances. She started the business with her main focus as a nail technicion.The vision for added makeup, cosmetic and the whole woman needs, has grown over the years from just "pretty nails"

    OUR MISSION

    J&J Cosmetic mission is to elevate, enable, approve and eventually bring back confidence in ladies around the globe through superb items that empower both inward and external beauty Our products are top of the line pure enriched organic extracts. Naturally produced from roots, flowers, herbs and essential oils. Our products are affordable and won,t beat you pocket. Therefore, join the family where all your beauty needs are met!!!

    OUR OBJECTIVES

    CEO and founder Teleata Mitchell, fell in love with the idea of beauty in women and the importance of taking care of one's appearance. in 2000 she desided to open an online cosmetic store. adding cosmetic manufacturing to having a main location in kingston. As the brand continues to grow the aim is to have a fully online platform. J&J Cosmetic offers quality products and services and specializes in customization to best suit the customers needs.

    WHAT WE DO -

    J&J Cosmetic has a relatively small but cute selection of beauty products. You can find eye shadows, powders, body highlighters. We sell authentic skincare products that support a healthy lifestyle and offer benefits beyond what general luxury brands do.
